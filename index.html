    SETUP GUIDE · TIPS · REFERENCE WEIGHTS
    
    
    
    
  
    

  
    

    
    
    
      
    QUICK SETUP FOR ±0.1g
    
      
    
        
    1. Place phone on a soft surface — mouse pad, folded cloth, or thick notebook. NOT a hard table.
    
        
    2. Phone face-up, flat, not tilted. Even 5° of tilt reduces accuracy.
    
        
    3. Not charging during use. Charging cable adds vibration and thermal drift.
    
        
    4. Wait 5 minutes after charging before measuring — let phone cool to ambient temperature.
    
        
    5. Press CAL → use a nickel (5.000g) as primary weight, dollar bill (1.00g) as 2nd point.
    
        
    6. Press VERIFY after each calibration — this trains the accuracy engine over time.
    
      
    
    
    

    
    
    
      
    SURFACE QUALITY — BIGGEST FACTOR
    
      
    
        
    ★★★★Foam mouse pad, 5+ layers cloth     Excellent
    
        
    ★★★☆Thick notebook, paperback book       Good
    
        
    ★★☆☆Magazine, thin paperback              OK
    
        
    ★☆☆☆Hard desk, countertop, glass         Poor
    
      
    
      
    Physics: the soft surface acts as a spring. Weight compresses it → phone tilts slightly → accelerometer detects the tilt change. Softer surface = more deflection = stronger signal = better accuracy.

    WHAT THE ACCURACY % MEANS
    
      
    
        The % is a real-time signal quality score — it rises when you are calibrated and the phone is perfectly still, and drops when the phone moves or is uncalibrated.

    
        The ~±Xg label under the bar is the precision estimate — the realistic gram-accuracy you can expect based on your calibration quality and surface. This is the number that really matters.
      
    
    
    

    
    
    
      
    TROUBLESHOOTING
    
      
    Display drifts with nothing on it: Thermal drift from charging. Unplug and wait 5 min, then re-TARE.
    
      
    Won't read below 1g: Surface too hard. Switch to a mouse pad or folded cloth.
    
      
    Accuracy worse than ±0.5g after cal: Add a second calibration point (use CAL again with a different weight).
    
      
    No sensor reading / always 0: Motion permission denied — check browser/site settings and reload.
    
      
    Noisy / jumping display: External vibration (fan, traffic). Move to a still location or use 0.1g PRECISION mode.
    
      
    iOS — no permission prompt: Open in Safari (not in-app browser). Tap Share → Add to Home Screen.
