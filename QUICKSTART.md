# Phoneway v4.1.1 - Quick Start

## 🚀 Live Now
**https://phoneway.vercel.app**

## 📱 Install in 10 Seconds

### iPhone/iPad
1. Open **Safari** → https://phoneway.vercel.app
2. Tap **Share** → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Done! Works offline.

### Android
1. Open **Chrome** → https://phoneway.vercel.app
2. Tap **"Install"** when prompted
3. Done! Works offline.

### Desktop
1. Open **Chrome/Edge** → https://phoneway.vercel.app
2. Click **⊕ Install** in address bar
3. Done! Desktop app installed.

## ⚖️ First Use

1. **Place phone** on a soft surface (mouse pad, folded cloth)
2. **Open the app** (tap the icon)
3. **Calibrate** with a nickel (5g) or dollar bill (1g)
4. **Start weighing!**

## 🎯 Accuracy
- With good calibration: **±0.2g**
- With excellent surface: **±0.1g**

## 🆘 Need Help?
- Calibration tips: Tap CAL button
- Check accuracy: Tap VERIFY button
- View stats: Tap STATS button
